Don't forget to active this plug-in in Adobe Lightroom
or it won't show up in the export list.

  1. Open the Lightroom Plug-in Manager

    In Lightroom choose File → Plug-in Manager…

  2. Activate the iPhoto plug-in

    Choose iPhoto in the left-hand pane and click the Enable button in the right-hand pane.
